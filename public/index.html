OutcomeLogic

OutcomeLogic synthesises clinical trial evidence in seconds — extracting PICO structure, endpoint data, risk of bias, and GRADE certainty from full-text papers, then generating both a clinician summary and a patient-facing shared decision-making report.

①

Upload or Search

Upload a PDF or enter a trial name or PMID — e.g. SANO trial or 32109013.

②

Extract

The 3-node consensus pipeline retrieves full text where available and extracts PICO, endpoints, risk of bias, and GRADE certainty.

③

Review & Export

The synthesis includes endpoint visualisation, critical appraisal, and a lay summary ready for shared decision-making. Save to the library or export as PDF.

Analysis is performed by a 3-node AI consensus pipeline on full-text where available, falling back to abstract. Results should be verified against the primary source before clinical use. Limited to 20 analyses per day per user.
